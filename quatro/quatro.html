Connect Four

VS

Rules:

  1. Players must connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row to win.
  2. Only one piece is played at a time.
  3. Players can be on the offensive or defensive.
  4. The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate.
  5. The starter player will be random

Tips:

The dimensions of the board are 7x6

On top of the board it is possible to see who is playing

It is also possible who is playing in the name (highlighted in green)

It is possible to win in 3 different ways and tie:

  • Horizontal
  • Vertical
  • Diagonal
  • Draw

When you finish the game, a window with the game information will appear.
